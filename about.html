About TriMap

An Isometric Perspective

Inspired by a reddit post, TriMap is an online Dungeons and Dragons map drawn in an isometric perspective. Add tiles, doors, and levels in an elegent 3D perspective, then place your characters on the map and explore. Use TriMap to keep track of players, NPCs, and other objects to free your mind to focus on what's important: the adventure.
